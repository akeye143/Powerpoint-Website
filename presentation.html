CONSISTENCY IS KEY TO A GREAT PRESENTATION

Consistency is the determining factor on building a clear, memorable and truts worthy presentation. You can do this with the help of engaging and Slide Master.


SLIDE MASTER

Slide master is a powerful PowerPoint feature that allows you to set a specific theme for your presentation and effortlessly makes changes.

By utilizing the full features of the slide master you gain access to the front, colour, layout,footer, watermarks, page number along with many other aspects.

The top slide other wise known as the master slide is arranged on a hierarchy so all changes made on the master slide affect all other slides. this is useful when you need to have set objectives applied to the slides to ensure the students are aware what is expected of them in lesson.

BENEFITS OF SLIDE MASTER

Time Saving
No longer need to waste time changing details slide by slide.

Unique
Can create a template that is unique to yourself

Reusable
Template can be re-used as many times as possible.

Professional
The consistency from the design completes the presentation and gives it a clean ‘look’.

Adaptable
can be easily changed, and any features on normal view do not affect the aspects on the master slide

Distributable
Templates can be shared with other teachers and educators.
